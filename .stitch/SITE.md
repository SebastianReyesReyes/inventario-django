# Site Vision: JMIE Inventory Management

A robust, modern, and high-precision inventory management system for IT assets. The application serves as the single source of truth for tracking, managing, and documenting corporate technology assets under strict legal regulations (Ley 21.663, Ley 21.719).

**Stitch Project ID:** projects/jmie-inventory-modern

## 1. Core Principles
- **Aesthetic Precision**: Every pixel communicates quality and reliability.
- **Legal Rigor**: Clear, professional layouts for document generation (Actas).
- **Efficiency-First**: Dense, well-organized dashboards and lists for high-speed operations.
- **Modern Interactions**: HTMX-driven fluid transitions and real-time feedback.

## 2. Project Goals
- Redesign the Dashboard with high-fidelity analytics cards and activity feeds.
- Polish the Inventory and Actas lists with advanced filtering and row actions.
- Standardize all forms and modals to the JMIE design system.
